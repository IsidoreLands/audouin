# How to Contribute to Audouin

We welcome contributions from everyone! Here's how you can help.

## Reporting Bugs

* Open a **New Issue** on our GitHub page.
* Please include your OS, Docker version, and `docker compose` version.
* Provide the full log output from `setup.sh` or `docker compose logs`.
* Describe the steps to reproduce the bug.

## Submitting Fixes or Features

1.  **Fork** the repository.
2.  Create a new branch for your feature (`git checkout -b feat/my-new-feature`).
3.  Make your changes.
4.  **Sign your commits** with your GPG key.
5.  Push your branch to your fork.
6.  Open a **Pull Request** against the `main` branch of our repository.
